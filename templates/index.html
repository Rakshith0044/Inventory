ABOUT

Connected supply chain is one of those technologies, which is focused on innovation for enhancing the experience of each stakeholder in the system. With the fast-evolving nature of digital transformation and globalization, a connected end-to-end supply chain aims to provide comprehensive visibility and a shared view across the manufacturing enterprise, suppliers, customers, outsource partners and other trading partners. Ensuring integrated supply chain operations and management will enable manufacturers to better anticipate and serve customer requirements and supply chain demand.
